NSS
NSS has been introduced in the year 1998-1999 in this Institution.
MOTTO OF NSS : “NOT ME BUT YOU “
FUNCTION OF NSS: The Focal Objectives of the scheme are
Development of the personality of students through Community service’. This ives is sought to be achieved by enabling the students to:
a) Understand the community where they work.
b) Understand themselves in relation with their community.
c) Identify the needs and problems in the community and they can find the solution for resolving the problem.
d) Develop among themselves a sense of social and civic responsibility.
e) Apply their technical knowledge in finding practical solutions to individual and community problems.
f) Develop competence required for group living and sharing responsibilities.
g) Gain skills in mobilizing community participation.
h) Acquire leadership qualities and democratic attitude.
i) Develop capacity to meet emergencies and natural disaster and
j) Practice National Integration
