ABOUT DEPARTMENT
What was luxury yesterday is a necessary today, so also with technologies computer science is one of the best branches in S J Polytechnic,widely accepted by the industry and Information Technology across the globe. Started in 1985, the Department has modern and latest infrastructure consisting of Linux Lab, Multimedia Lab, Internet web facility for both students and staff and above all the need of the industry is met, in a conducive manner.

This department has fully fledged, well qualified faculty committed to fullfil the industry requirement all over the world. In addition continuing education programs relating to the subjects for staff and the students are conducted. The student in the final year are gained to upgrade their Communication and presentation skill, to face interviews with confidence and aplomb and to be natural leaders in whichever field they aspire to go to, by identifying and lapping their inherent talent so as to be among the best in the business. Most of them are well placed in reputed Organizations like INFOSYS, ACCENTURE, HP, FLEXTRONICS, TCS, IBM, CISCO.

MISSION
M1: To provide fundamental computing technical knowledge and to enhance the same through best practices of teaching learning process M2: Transform individuals as responsible professionals and entrepreneurs by providing quality soft skills training that meets the needs of industry M3: To instill interpersonal skills and sense of ethical responsibility
