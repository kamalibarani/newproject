Sri Jayachamarajendra(Govt) Polytechnic

Sri Jayachamarajendra (Govt) Polytechnic, Bangalore is a premier technical institute started by the great visionary and statesman Sir M Visvesvaraya for providing value based technical education. It is situated on a sprawling campus of 10 acres and is the largest polytechnic is South East Asia. Sri Jayachamarajendra(Govt) Polytechnic offers 14 courses in various conventional and diversified areas of technology.


QUICK FACTS

1. Located in the heart of the high tech city Bangalore.
2. Hostel accomodation is available for boys on campus.
3. Assuring 100% placement for all the eligible candidates.
4. 3 NCC wings - army, navy and airforce for both boys and girls.
5. NSS for the development of the personality of students through Community Service.
6. Excellent sporting infrastructure with participation at all levels.
7. Continuing Education Cell (CCTEK) for imparting short/full term training programmes for faculty and students.
8. Women in development (WID) project for the empowerment of women.
